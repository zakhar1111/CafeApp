# Restaurant POS – Short Requirements
1. The restaurant manages multiple tables, each with a unique table number and seating capacity.
2. Staff members can log into the system to manage orders, bills, and payments.
3. Staff can register customer arrivals, including table, party size, and arrival time.
4. Customers can place orders containing one or more food and drink items.
5. Menu items can include predefined modifications (e.g., No Cheese, Extra Meat).
6. The system tracks order status (e.g., Reviewing Menu, Waiting for Order, Served).
7. Staff can generate bills showing ordered items, prices, and total amount due.
8. The system records when a bill has been issued to a customer.
9. Staff can apply manual bill adjustments, such as discounts or item removals.
10. Customers can make one or multiple payments for the same bill (split payments supported).
11. The system supports table reservations made by phone, in person, or online, storing customer name, reservation date/time, and party size.
